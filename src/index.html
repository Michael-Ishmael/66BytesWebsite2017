About

66 Bytes is an accomplished software company with a mission to offer, rapid, targeted solutions to clients of all sizes. We take on projects with clear, achievable goals and aim to deliver results within a small time-frame.

It is our belief that software solutions, whilst sophisticated, should never be complicated. We have found that any challenge can be broken into small deliverable parts that start to offer value without any unpredictable expense.

Our team is composed of mature, multi-discipline developers whose collective experience covers many projects across a spectrum of companies and industries. Consequently, we are able to offer a range of blended technical services.

Whether it be consultancy or green-field development, troubleshooting legacy applications or integrating existing systems with new ones, we can deliver a solution, always agreed using open and honest communication that never gets lost in technical jargon.
